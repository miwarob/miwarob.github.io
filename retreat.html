The Retreat

A Mini Retreat Space in Greenville, SC

A peaceful tiny house tucked into a quiet corner of San Souci — available to rent by the hour or for overnight stays by inquiry. A place to pause, breathe, and reset before or after your bodywork session, or as a stand-alone mini retreat.

Mayu: The Cocoon of Transformation

This tiny house was the passion project of my twenties. She is custom-designed and built, and her name is Mayu (Ma-you), which means cocoon in Japanese. She is the perfect little quiet getaway — whether it’s an escape from busy mom/dad life, a change of scene to do some quiet reading or writing, or a spot to meditate, take a nap, or have a heart-to-heart with yourself or someone else.

Ideal for a single person or a couple, the space has a full shower/bath and a fancy compost toilet. The kitchen has a fridge, sink, and electric tea kettle, along with mugs, tea, silverware, and dishware. There’s even a small retro three-burner propane stove available for longer overnight stays, and a tiny wood stove for chilly winter nights. With a cozy day bed, a pull-out desk, and a full-sized queen mattress in the loft, there are plenty of spaces to settle in and relax.

This is a space that wants to be used and enjoyed. If you feel it would support you to have it for a few hours before or after your bodywork session, please take advantage! If you have another idea of how you’d like to use the space — perhaps for a solo weekend retreat — propose your idea and let’s make it happen. Fresh, healthy food from nearby Sun Belly Cafe could be waiting for you in the fridge. Or a bottle of wine and a box of chocolates. The possibilities are endless.

This is your custom mini retreat. Allow Mayu to be your Cocoon of Transformation.
